## How Credits Work

Every API call costs **1 credit**. WebSocket messages also cost 1 credit each. Checking your usage (`/v1/account/usage`) is free.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Monthly Credits | Overage | Rate Limit |
|------|-------|----------------|---------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 100 (one-time) | N/A | 10 req/min |
| **Starter** | $19/mo | 5,000 | $0.005/credit | 30 req/min |
| **Pro** | $49/mo | 30,000 | $0.003/credit | 120 req/min |
| **Power** | $149/mo | 150,000 | $0.002/credit | 500 req/min |

## Credit Reset

Credits **reset** at the start of each billing cycle — they do not accumulate. However, up to **20% of unused credits** roll over to the next month (capped at 120% of your plan's allocation).

**Example:** Pro plan (30,000 credits). You use 20,000 in March. April balance:
```
30,000 (new grant) + 2,000 (20% of 10,000 unused) = 32,000
```

## Overage

If you exceed your monthly credits, additional usage is charged at your plan's overage rate. Overage is billed automatically on your next invoice.

## Checking Your Balance

```bash
curl -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_KEY" \
  "https://api.tickr.dev/v1/account/usage"
```

```json
{
  "credits_remaining": 28500,
  "credits_used_this_period": 1500,
  "tier": "pro",
  "rate_limit": 120,
  "period_end": "2026-05-07T00:00:00Z"
}
```

## What Happens When Credits Run Out

- REST API returns `403 Insufficient credits`
- WebSocket connection closes with code `1008`
- You can upgrade your plan or wait for the next billing cycle
